Marie Kondo

Japanese tidying up expert

Marie Kondo is a popular Japanese tidying up expert. She created the KonMari Method of cleaning which people clean room by room, picking up each object and deciding whether it sparks joy. From a young age Kondo has loved cleaning and spent five years as a Shinto Shrine attend maid. While in school at Tokyo’s Woman’s Christian University, at 19 years old had started her own tidying consultant business. She has published four books about the art of organization and stars in the Netflix original series Tidying Up with Marie Kondo. She is also the founder of KonMari Media, Inc.

Mindy Kaling

American actress, comedian, producer, and writer

Vera Mindy Chokalingam, known to most as Mindy Kaling, was born in Cambridge, Massachusetts after her Indian father and Bangladesh mother had met in Nigeria and immigrated to the US. Kaling attended college at Dartmouth where she was actively involved with improv comedy group and writer for the college humor magazine. After college, Kaling moved to Brooklyn, New York where she shortly worked as a production assistant and performed standup comedy. In 2004, Kaling was hired by the producer of The Office as a writer and performer. At the time, Kaling was only 24 and the only women of eight person writing staff. Kaling played Kelly Kapoor, a sassy and chatty customer service representative in the show. She also wrote and produced her own romantic comedy called The Mindy Project. Kaling has written two successful memoirs, Is Everyone Hanging Without me? and Why Not Me?, and for many other shows such as Champions and Four Weddings and a Funeral.
