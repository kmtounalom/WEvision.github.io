Sadaf Monajemi

Innovator

Sadaf Monajemi is the cofounder and director of See-Mode Technologies, an AI service clinicians to predict strokes more accurately without costly additional tests. See-Mode collects medical images in order to tell the important risk factors to providing a reliable prediction. Monajemi previously focused on machine learning and medical application of AI at the National University of Singapore where she also received her PhD.
